# Goalkeeper Tactics Quiz

A browser-based flashcard quiz for youth goalkeepers, built around real match scenarios with coaching explanations. Designed for 14-year-old keepers playing at a competitive level — all scenarios reflect youth football realities, not adult/professional situations.

## How to use

1. Open `index.html` in any web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. No internet connection required — everything runs locally
3. No installation, accounts, or setup needed

## Starting a quiz

On the main menu you can:

- **Filter by category** — click any category card to toggle it on/off. At least one must stay selected.
- **Choose a mode:**
  - *All Questions* — all 48 questions in your selected categories
  - *Quick 10* — 10 random questions (good for a quick session)
  - *Match 20* — 20 random questions (a solid training block)
  - *Full 30* — 30 random questions (a proper workout)
- Click **Start Quiz** when ready

## Playing

- Read the situation, then pick the best answer from the four options
- After answering, a coaching explanation appears explaining why the correct answer is right (and why the others fall short)
- A tip at the bottom gives a practical rule or principle to remember
- Use **Next Question** to advance, or **Skip** to push a question to the end of the queue
- Your correct/wrong/streak counts update live at the top

## End screen

After finishing, you get:
- An overall score and grade (Elite / Strong / Developing / Keep Training)
- A category-by-category breakdown showing where you are strongest and where to focus

| Category | Questions | What it covers |
|---|---|---|
| Positioning | 10 | Angle play, adjusting with the ball, crosses, depth off your line, 2v1s, muddy pitch adjustments |

| Shot Stopping | 8 | Dive technique, low barrier, parrying direction, sightlines, recovery saves, penalties |
| Set Pieces | 7 | Corner positioning, indirect free kicks, long throws, organising before delivery, staying alert on attacking set pieces |
| Sweeper-Keeper | 6 | Reading through balls, changing your mind mid-sweep, feet skills outside the box, building confidence after mistakes |
| Communication | 8 | Calling crosses, resetting after goals, proactive scanning, helping build-up play, managing the defensive line |
| Decision Making | 10 | 1v1 stance, backpass pressure, game management, mental toughness, concentration, handling nerves |

## Tips for getting the most out of it

- **Don't just click through** — read the explanation even when you get it right. The coaching tip is the valuable part.
- **Use category filters** to drill a weak area. If set pieces let you down in a game, run a set pieces-only session.
- **Play it more than once** — questions are shuffled each time so the order changes. With 48 questions, even the "Full 30" mode won't show you everything in one go.
- **Talk through the explanations** with your coach or a parent. The reasoning behind each answer is as important as the answer itself.
- **After a match**, use the quiz to revisit situations that came up — filter by the relevant category and see if your in-game decisions match the coaching advice.
